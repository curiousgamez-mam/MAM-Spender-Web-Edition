Important: How Cookies Work

          
Your MAM session cookie is tied to your IP address.

          
Your IP may change when your router restarts, your device disconnects or reconnects to your ISP, or your ISP rotates addresses automatically.

          
When your IP changes, your cookie becomes invalid. This is normal behavior. If the app reports a session error, simply create a new cookie.


          
Step 1: Create A Valid Cookie

          
You must do this on the same device that runs MAM Spender Web.

          
    
            
  1. Log into myanonamouse.net.
    2. 
            
  2. Open Menu, then Preferences, then Security.
    3. 
            
  3. Under Active Sessions, keep the entry that says "log out". This is your current session. Remove any other entries.
    4. 
            
  4. Copy your IP address shown there. It will look something like XX.XXX.XXX.XX (XXXXX).
    5. 
            
  5. Scroll to Create Session.
    6. 
            
  6. Select the radio option "ASN locked".
    7. 
            
  7. Paste your IP address into the IP field. Be careful of trailing spaces.
    8. 
            
  8. Click Create.
    9. 
            
  9. Copy the long string that appears. This is your cookie value.
    10. 
          


          
Step 2: Create Or Update Cookie File

          
Option A: Use the Mam Session_ID panel in this app. Paste the Session_ID string, choose where to save it, and click Save Session_ID. Recommended file name: MAM.cookies.

          
Option B: Replace an existing cookie file. Open your existing .cookies file, replace its contents with the new cookie string, and save the file.

          
Keep this file private. Anyone with it can use your session.
